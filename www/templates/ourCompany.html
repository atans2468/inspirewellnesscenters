Our co-founders, Gary Holland and Craig Johnson have been friends for 30+ years and business partners for 5 years. They are two of the co-founders of UnionVillage of Henderson, NV., the “largest healthcare project in the United States” according to Healthcare Design Magazine.


Over the past five years the company has been designing an integrated wellness approach to Senior Living. The first of which is in planning at UnionVillage – a 300 unit $60M project.


The company has also consulted with one of the largest and best healthcare delivery systems in the United States with the goal being to bring a senior wellness strategy to the organization.
